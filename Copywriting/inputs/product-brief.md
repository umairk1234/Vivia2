## What It Is

Intimate Bloom is a topical estriol cream that rebuilds vaginal tissue and restores natural moisture from the inside out.

Unlike over-the-counter lubricants that only mask symptoms, estriol actually **heals the tissue thinning and dryness** that happens during menopause.

---

## How It's Different

### vs Lubricants (KY, Astroglide, etc.)
- Lubes = temporary coating, doesn't fix the problem
- Intimate Bloom = actually restores tissue

### vs Systemic HRT (pills, patches)
- Systemic HRT = floods whole body with hormones, risks
- Intimate Bloom = local action only, stays in the tissue

### vs Supplements (sea buckthorn, etc.)
- Supplements = indirect, slow, questionable efficacy
- Intimate Bloom = direct hormonal restoration

### vs Other vaginal estrogens (Premarin, Vagifem)
- Stronger estrogens = more systemic absorption, more risk
- Estriol = gentlest estrogen, minimal systemic absorption

## The European Angle

Estriol has been safely used in Europe **for over 50 years** as an over-the-counter solution. It's what European doctors have trusted for generations.

In the US, it's not FDA approved (because no one submitted for approval — can't patent a bioidentical hormone). American women are denied access to what European women get easily.

## Usage Notes for Copywriting

**Lead with:**
- Her pain (emotional and physical)
- The system that failed her
- The European solution she's been denied

**Not with:**
- Product features
- Ingredient science
- Clinical jargon

**Build trust through:**
- Doctor credibility
- European standard
- Real women's stories
- Understanding her specific experience

**Address objections:**
- "Is it safe?" → Gentlest estrogen, local only, 50 years in Europe
- "Will it cause cancer?" → Different from systemic HRT, minimal absorption
- "Do I need a prescription?" → Gray area, we ship direct
- "Will it interact with my meds?" → Local action, doesn't affect systemic
